The Three Video Games I Have Observed and Investigated

The Top 3 Video Games Worth Research

Metal Gear Solid V: The Phantom Pain

This video game contains various aspects to investigate. It can start from historical perspective - mostly found in it - and semiotic aspects such as the use of colors, sounds, and also movements. It can also be observed using film or cinema studies.

Mark of the Ninja

This video game is included into the remnants of past video games. Yet, this game has very satisfying visual graphics and game mechanics. This video game also offers uniques game mechanics where the character is set to depart to missions but in the end it has to choose who to sacrifice: himself or his entire clan.

Naruto Shippuden: Ultimate Ninja Storm 4

This video game is the adaptation from the anime Naruto Shippuden. This video game is combat style game with stories directly taken from the original story.
